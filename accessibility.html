Accessibility Statement

Last updated:

SEO Renewal is committed to making seorenewal.com usable by as many people as possible, including those who rely on assistive technologies. We aim to meet the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.2, Level AA across our core pages.

Assistive options on this site

Every page includes an “Accessibility options” button (bottom-right on desktop, bottom-center on mobile). It is fully keyboard operable and opens a panel where you can:

  • Increase or decrease text size (110%–200%) and adjust line spacing.
  • Switch to High Contrast or Dark themes, or return to the light default.
  • Always underline links, or highlight links, for color-independent reading.
  • Switch to a dyslexia-friendly font (Atkinson Hyperlegible).
  • Turn on a movable reading guide (use the mouse or Up/Down arrow keys).
  • Enable a big cursor and a high-contrast focus ring.
  • Reduce motion (also honors your operating system’s “reduce motion” setting).

Your choices are saved on your own device only (in local storage). We do not use cookies or any tracking to store these settings.

Built-in accessibility

  • “Skip to main content / navigation / footer” links appear when you start tabbing.
  • Semantic landmarks (header, navigation, main, footer) and a single, descriptive H1 per page.
  • Visible keyboard focus indicators on all interactive elements.
  • The accessibility menu is a focus-trapped dialog that closes with Esc and returns focus to its button.

Important note

An accessibility menu and overlay aid access but do not, on their own, make a website fully compliant. Our accessibility work also includes underlying WCAG 2.2 AA fixes (keyboard support, focus visibility, color contrast, labels, headings, and reflow).

Known limitations

  • When you click through to our partner (Stan Ventures) to complete a managed-SEO request, that third-party site is outside our control and is governed by its own accessibility practices.
  • Video captioning/transcription and remediation of legacy PDFs are not part of the current scope.

Feedback & contact

If you encounter an accessibility barrier on this site, please let us know and we’ll work to fix it. Contact us at support@seorenewal.com. We aim to respond within five business days.

Measures & assessment

This site is tested with automated tooling (axe-core) and manual keyboard and screen-reader checks. We conduct a quarterly mini-audit and address issues on an ongoing basis.
